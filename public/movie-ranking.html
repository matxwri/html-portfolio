The Best Anime Series According to Matt

My Top 3 Anime of All Time

1. Sword Art Online

Sword Art Online was the very first anime that I had ever completed.
I was mesmorized by the story as well as the characters within it,
and it easily became one of my all-time favorites. A must-watch for sure!

2. Tokyo Ghoul

Tokyo Ghoul was an anime that a friend might have mentioned that I
watch and I eventually did. The introduction was very innocent-like
and then BOOM!, everything turns to chaos. The emotion within this series
kept me intrested through to the end and it will always be in my top favorites.

3. Demon Slayer

Packed with action and fighting throughout the entire series, Demon Slayer is
an anime that you can't take your eyes off. Phenomonal storyline with breathtaking
artstyle, everything about this anime is simply just amazing. I was very disappointed
to see it end and am hoping for more in the future.
